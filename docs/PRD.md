
# KitchenKart - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Version
1.0

## Project Vision
KitchenKart is a smart household management app that helps families organize groceries, pantry items, shopping lists, budgets, and meal planning in one place.

## Mission
To become India's easiest and smartest home management app.

## Target Users
- Families
- Homemakers
- Working professionals
- Students
- Senior citizens

## Core Features

### Dashboard
- Home summary
- Low stock alerts
- Monthly budget
- Shopping reminders

### Shopping List
- Add/Edit/Delete items
- Category-wise organization
- Quantity management
- Family sharing

### Pantry
- Current stock
- Expiry tracking
- Low stock alerts

### Budget
- Monthly grocery budget
- Spending analysis
- Savings tracker

### Shop Online
- Open grocery apps
- Save shopping lists

### Family Sharing
- Shared pantry
- Shared shopping lists

### Meal Planner
- Weekly meal planning
- Auto-generate shopping lists

## Future Features
- Barcode scanner
- Voice input
- AI recommendations
- Bill scanner
- Price comparison

## Technology
- Flutter
- SQLite
- Firebase (future)

## Goal
Launch Version 1 on the Google Play Store.
