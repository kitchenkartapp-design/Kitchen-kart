# KitchenKart Features

## MVP (Version 1)

### Dashboard
- Home summary
- Low stock alerts
- Monthly budget
- Shopping reminders

### Shopping List
- Add, edit and delete items
- Category-wise organization
- Quantity management

### Pantry
- Current stock
- Expiry date tracking
- Low stock alerts

### Budget
- Monthly grocery budget
- Spending history
- Savings tracker

### Meal Planner
- Weekly meal planner
- Auto-generate shopping list

### Family Sharing
- Shared pantry
- Shared shopping list
